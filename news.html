Merge With Velocity Music

May 15, 2024

We are thrilled to announce a major milestone in the evolution of Soulscape Records. As of today, we are officially merging with Velocity Music, a distinguished division of TheVMedia. This union represents not just a strategic alignment, but a powerful synergy that will elevate the Electronic Dance Music (EDM) scene to new heights.

__________________________________________________________

A Fusion of Excellence

Soulscape Records has always been about pushing the boundaries of EDM, nurturing talent, and delivering cutting-edge music that resonates with fans around the globe. Our journey has been one of innovation, passion, and a relentless commitment to quality. By merging with Velocity Music, we are joining forces with a label that shares our vision and amplifies our mission. Velocity Music, under the umbrella of TheVMedia, has established itself as a beacon of creativity and influence in the music industry. Known for its diverse roster of artists and a deep-rooted connection with the EDM community, Velocity Music brings a wealth of experience and a dynamic platform that will enhance our ability to discover, produce, and promote top-tier EDM tracks.

__________________________________________________________

What This Means for Our Artists and Fans

For our artists, this merger opens up a world of new opportunities. They will benefit from increased resources, expanded marketing capabilities, and access to a broader audience through TheVMedia's extensive network. Our commitment to artist development remains steadfast, and we are excited about the innovative collaborations and projects that will emerge from this partnership. Our fans, who are the heartbeat of Soulscape Records, can look forward to a richer, more diverse music experience. The merger will enable us to release more frequent and varied content, host larger and more immersive events, and engage with the EDM community in unprecedented ways. Together with Velocity Music, we aim to set new standards for what is possible in the world of electronic dance music.

__________________________________________________________

Looking Ahead

This merger is more than just a business decision; it is a creative convergence that promises to redefine the EDM landscape. We are committed to preserving the unique identity and spirit of Soulscape Records while embracing the fresh perspectives and resources that Velocity Music brings. Our shared goal is to create an unparalleled music experience that inspires and moves people.

Stay tuned for more updates as we unveil new music, events, and collaborations. The future of EDM is bright, and together with Velocity Music, Soulscape Records is ready to lead the charge. Welcome to a new era of electronic dance music. Welcome to the future with Soulscape Records and Velocity Music.
